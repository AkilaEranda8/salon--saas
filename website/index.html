Platform Features

Everything your salon needs,
in one platform

Built specifically for salons — from single-chair studios to multi-branch chains.

Smart Appointments

Visual calendar with drag-and-drop scheduling, multi-service bookings, recurring appointments, and online booking portal for customers.

Multi-Branch Management

Manage all your salon locations from a single dashboard. Per-branch reports, staff assignments, services, and inventory — all unified.

Staff & Attendance

Track attendance, schedules, and leave. Automatic commission calculations, monthly earnings reports, and staff-level performance insights.

Customer Loyalty & Memberships

Reward your regulars with a points-based loyalty program. Create memberships, service bundles, and exclusive packages to boost retention.

AI Chat Assistant

An intelligent AI bot that answers customer questions, helps with booking queries, and gives you business insights — available 24/7.

KPI Dashboard & Reports

Revenue trends, appointment analytics, staff performance, and customer behaviour — all in beautiful, exportable reports and live dashboards.

Inventory Management

Track product stock levels, get low-stock alerts, and record inventory usage per service. Stay in control of your salon supplies and costs.

SMS & Marketing Automation

Send automated appointment reminders, birthday offers, and promotional SMS campaigns. Reduce no-shows and keep customers coming back.

Walk-in Queue & Consent Forms

Digital walk-in queue management so customers wait comfortably, plus digital consent forms to keep client records professional and compliant.
